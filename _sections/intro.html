Hi! I'm Ed van Veen, an onco-neuroscientist at UCLA.

I'm interested in how cancer interacts with the central and peripheral nervous systems. This includes how cancer therapies affect brain controlled physiology and how tumor evolution selects for cancers that communicate bidirectionally with the nervous system.
