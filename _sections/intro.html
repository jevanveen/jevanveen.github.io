I'm Ed van Veen, an onco-neuroscientist at UCLA.

I'm investigating how the nervous system, cancer, and cancer therapies interact. We use mice as a model because of the sophisticated molecular tools available, and the ability to recapitulate human carcinogenesis using engineered mutations.
