I'm Ed van Veen, an onco-neuroscientist at UCLA.

I'm investigating how the nervous system, cancer, and cancer therapies interact. We study this problem in a tridirectional fashion because in humans, tumors continue to evolve in the face of treatment with chemotherapy.
