The van Veen lab at UCLA.

Our team is investigating how the nervous system, cancer, and cancer therapies interact. We use mice as a model because of the sophisticated molecular tools available for observing and perturbing neural function, and because mice develop tumors and respond to chemotherapy in ways strikingly similar to humans.
