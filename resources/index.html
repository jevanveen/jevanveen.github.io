Webservers for single-cell studies

Single-cell RNA sequencing has revolutionized our understanding of cellular heterogeneity, but making sense of these datasets requires approachable visualization tools. Below are the interactive webservers we maintain so you can explore hormone-responsive cell populations without writing code.

Heterogeneity of the ventromedial hypothalamus (2020)
Query genes of interest to explore their expression patterns in our ventromedial hypothalamus dataset.
Tamoxifen response explorer (2021)
Investigate differential expression across neural and glial cell types in response to tamoxifen treatment using our interactive viewer.
